## FAQ

**Q: Can I submit late?**  
A: Yes! Submit anytime. Late submissions are still eligible for badges.

**Q: What if my code doesn't work perfectly?**  
A: That's okay! We value effort and learning. Document issues and what you tried.

**Q: Can I use different platforms (AWS, Azure)?**  
A: Absolutely! Adapt the requirements to your platform.

**Q: Do I need to attend live sessions?**  
A: Not required, but recommended. Recordings are available.

**Q: Can I work with others?**  
A: Yes! Collaboration is encouraged. Just document who you worked with.

**Q: How long do badges stay valid?**  
A: Forever! They're part of your learning journey.
